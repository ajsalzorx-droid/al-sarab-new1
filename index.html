About Al Sarab Cafeteria

Serving Happiness Since 1988

From Al Sarab Shahba to a trusted UAE food brand

Al Sarab Cafeteria began its journey in 1988 as Al Sarab Shahba. Built on a passion for food and a family tradition of quality cooking, we have grown into a trusted food brand across the UAE.

We serve fresh, tasty, and high-quality food prepared with carefully selected ingredients. Our mission is to deliver delicious meals without compromising on taste, hygiene, or customer satisfaction.

Today, Al Sarab continues to grow with multiple outlets and sub-brands, aiming to expand from 23 stores to 50 stores by 2030.
